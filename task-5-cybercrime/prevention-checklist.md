# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

As college students, we spend most of our time online for classes, shopping, and hanging out. Here are 8 simple things you should always do to protect yourself from cyber frauds and scams.

### Financial & UPI Safety 
- [ ] **Never use your PIN to receive money:** Remember that your UPI PIN is only used when you are sending money or checking your balance. You never need to enter it to receive a payment.
- [ ] **Decline unknown collect requests:** If you get a random popup on GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm asking you to pay or approve a request from someone you don't know, decline it and block the user immediately.

### Account Security 
- [ ] **Turn on 2FA:** Enable Two-Factor Authentication on your WhatsApp, Instagram, and Gmail. This way, even if someone guesses your password, they still can't log in.
- [ ] **Don't reuse passwords:** Keep a different password for your college VTOP portal, your bank account, and your social media. If one gets hacked, the others stay safe.

### Browsing & Mobile Safety 
- [ ] **Avoid public Wi-Fi for payments:** Never pay your semester fees or do online shopping while connected to free campus or cafe Wi-Fi. Always switch to your mobile data.
- [ ] **Ignore sketchy links:** Don't click on random WhatsApp forwards or SMS links claiming you won an iPhone, a free recharge, or that a delivery package is stuck.
- [ ] **Download official apps only:** Only install apps from the official Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Avoid downloading "cracked" software or modded APKs, as they often contain malware.
- [ ] **Keep your socials private:** Set your Instagram and Facebook accounts to private. Don't accept requests from complete strangers who might try to steal your photos or identity.

###  Where to Report Cybercrime
If you or your friends ever get scammed or face online harassment, report it immediately. Don't feel embarrassed to ask for help.
* **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* **Helpline Number:** Dial **1930**
