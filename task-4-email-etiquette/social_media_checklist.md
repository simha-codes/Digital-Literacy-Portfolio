### Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist ###

## Do’s:- ##

-->Use respectful and professional language

-->Verify information before sharing

-->Maintain privacy settings

-->Share educational and positive content

-->Think before posting

## Don’ts :- ##

-->Do not share personal information publicly

-->Avoid offensive or inappropriate comments

-->Do not spread fake news or rumors

-->Avoid posting during academic hours

-->Do not engage in online arguments
